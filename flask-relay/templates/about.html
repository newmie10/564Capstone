About Meridian

Founded in 2021 by a team of former platform engineers, Meridian was born from a simple frustration: the tools available for operational analytics either required a small army to maintain or charged like one.

We're a distributed team across three time zones, backed by a modest seed round, focused on shipping a product that respects both your data and your engineering hours.

What we believe

  • Defaults matter more than features.
  • If a query takes thirty seconds, it won't get run.
  • Vendor lock-in is a tax. Open formats win.
